Remove-CIVApp

Synopsis

This cmdlet removes vApps from the cloud.

Syntax

Remove-CIVApp [-VApp] <CIVApp[]> [-RunAsync] [-Server <CIServer[]>] [-InformationAction <ActionPreference>] [-InformationVariable <String>] [-PipelineVariable <String>] [-WhatIf] [-Confirm] [<CommonParameters>]

This cmdlet removes vApps from the cloud.
