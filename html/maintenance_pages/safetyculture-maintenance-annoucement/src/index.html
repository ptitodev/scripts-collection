Scheduled maintenance downtime

November 13th, 2016

With growing demand for SafetyCulture worldwide, we’re scaling up to increase our database capacity.

What it means to you:

  • There will be a scheduled downtime from 03:00-06:00 UTC, 13th November. Your device running iAuditor won’t be able to sync to SafetyCulture during the maintenance downtime.
  • You can still use iAuditor to conduct inspections. Your audit data will stay on your device until the maintenance time is over and sync safely to your SafetyCulture account.
  • We expect normal service to resume no later than 6:00 UTC.

Key timezones:

  • Brisbane, QLD, Australia 13:00-16:00, Sunday 13th November
  • Kansas City, MO, USA 20:00- 23:00, Saturday 12th November
  • London, UK 02:00-05:00, Sunday 13th November
